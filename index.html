Instructions

Objective

Your aim is to guess the computer's secret code within up to 10 attempts.

The computer randomly generates a secret code of four colored pegs from six available colors, allowing for repeats.

Gameplay

The player makes a guess by selecting a sequence of four colored pegs from the available colors and submits it.

Repeated colors are allowed in the guess.

Repeats are allowed.

The computer provides feedback after each guess.

Feedback consists of a series of smaller pegs or markers placed adjacent to the guess.

Results

Black pegs indicate a guessed peg that is the correct color and in the correct position.

Gray pegs indicate a guessed peg that is the correct color but in the wrong position.

Winning

The player wins by guessing the code within the allotted attempts.

The computer wins if the player fails to guess the code.
